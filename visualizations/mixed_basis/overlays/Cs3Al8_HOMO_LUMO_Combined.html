Understanding the Visualization

HOMO (Highest Occupied Molecular Orbital) The highest energy orbital containing electrons. Shows where electrons currently reside and indicates potential nucleophilic sites.
LUMO (Lowest Unoccupied Molecular Orbital) The lowest energy unoccupied orbital. Indicates where incoming electrons would go and reveals electrophilic sites.
Orbital Overlap Significance Regions where HOMO and LUMO overlap indicate possible bonding interactions and are critical for understanding molecular reactivity.
